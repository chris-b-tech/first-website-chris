1.7 Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects

GDPR is a data protection act which requires businesses to protect the personal data and privacy of EU citizens for any type of transaction in the EU member states.
The regulation exists as a framework for laws across the continent and replaced the previous 1995 data protection directive.
The GDPR also regulates the exportation of personal data outside the EU.
Breaching this will result in breaking the law, this could be a fine.
